# Summary 

- This project looks at trends in U.S. Business Applications using data from the U.S. Census Bureaus Business Formation Statistics. It studies how new business formation has changed by state and region over time and useing regression models to predict future activity through 2027. The goal is to better understand regional business growth patterns, visualize the trends, and estimate what future business formation might look like across the country. 

# File Descriptions

- bfs_county_apps_annual.xlsx is the dataset
- dsp.ipynb is the notebook running the tests and displaying the results
